EGI EEG-fMRI setup cheat sheet

originally edited by Kathleen Wills 10Feb17

The day before, see if amp is charged by unplugging power cable and see if light flashes

To charge the amp: plug in the power, and turn the main switch on, check that the charge light is on.

Before the subject arrives, move the EGI cart into the scan room

EEG setup

  • Turn on monitor computer, password is: geodesic [lowercase]
  • Open firefox, type in ’10.10.10.51’ to check signal on amp

  • Plug in clock sync orange cable [with black tip] + data cable [with white tip] + sys clck from the wall to the tan box

  • prep solution & cap – see EGI instructions

  • while the cap is soaking [with sensors facing in], go to the computer and open app -hook up egi computer
  • check in-room cable connections:
  • Black box : orange data cable to amp
  • Ethernet cable to laptop, right port
    • check on black box for the ports to light up -Amp – is on physically (switch back of amp)
    • by software, launch firefox (10.10.10.51)

Capping

  • place cap on subject – see EGI instructions and refresher video
  • get hands as wide as possible in the cap, then have your hands facing the ground to adjust, thumbs should be between 8, 14 and 21, 25 sensors
  • put the spandage on
  • cut hole for a face and tie on top, make sure to leave enough spandage for head
  • hook up cap – plug into amp, twist the knob shut, click on top left of the Net system to turn ON -look at impedances at Ohm tab, fix ones that look bad [if in rush, a few (~4) can be yellow, but never any red] -hook up EEG leads.

Scan setup

Move subject into scanner

  • move cap/subject, amp, to scan room
  • stop measuring close to Ohm impendence, turn off app
  • turn off amp (otherwise will have to restart software), unplug all cords to amp
  • scan room [make sure 20ch base is on]
  • move chair to right side of bed, put amp on it
  • connect clock synch and eeg data cable to amp in scan room
    • connect subject leads, should come out beside the subject or by shoulder
    • attach physio on index finger and give pt button box
    • make sure amp no closer to machine than the divot on the base of the bed

Put person in:

  • raise the bed and align the subject
  • to align the subject, turn on cross hair + button, then move the subject until the crosshair is between their eybrows
  • landmark the subject by pressing the round dial once
  • send the person into the scanner by pressing the round dial a second time (long press) -turn the amp back on before leaving the room

Close 3T door.

Back in the console room:

-plug in EGI data cable into the black box and clock synch fibre optic from the waveguide and bnc [clock cable 2], tr trigger - the tr trigger is the brown cable and gets plugged into the + OUT on trigger interface under counter -check firefox again to see if amp has warmed up - look at impedances to make sure they haven’t changed

Start scans

Registering pt on MRI console

  • Check both 1st gradient boxes
  • EEG-fMRI
  • ETPB Repeat

  • Brain

  • rest scans, click the record red button on NET before start sequence on MRI console

Remove patient from the scanner:

  • Press HOME button to auto move table out
  • Unplug the EEG cap from the amp
  • Remove top half of coil and the physio attachments from the subject
  • Have patient sit up and get off the bed

  • Unplug amp and cords connections to it

  • remove cap and disinfect

  • see EGI instructions: removal disinfection

When done

  • move amp back to cart
  • unplug EGI data cable from hole in the wall, and OUT cable, and clock signal
  • remember to plug amp back in and make sure that it is charging.
  • copy data to memory stick
  • transfer data to helix along with physio data

TROUBLESHOOTING:

  • If need to quit NET on the monitor, force quit the app
  • To see if person is awake and using the button box, look at the top of the recording screen for selection on monitor
  • Plug laptop into the projector
  • Confirm the projector is on with the ON/OFF switch on the white box under the counter—press and hold until light comes up
  • Laptop with tasks needs tr trigger which is white USB cable from below counter, with “button box” sticker; tab to the instruction screen; THEN scan should trigger both computers
  • Autoconfig, then BGYRT letters setting. Use the dial to scroll and click in to select
